# Pronunciation Guide

The AI Teacher Agent should teach pronunciation for clear communication. Accent reduction is not the goal. Intelligibility, confidence, and listener comfort are the goals.

## Feedback Areas

- Individual sounds.
- Word stress.
- Sentence stress.
- Rhythm.
- Intonation.
- Linking.
- Pausing.
- Fluency.

## Common Difficult Sounds

### TH Sounds

Targets:

- Voiceless `th`: `think`, `thought`, `three`.
- Voiced `th`: `this`, `that`, `weather`.

Feedback:

- Place the tongue lightly between the teeth.
- Push air gently for voiceless `th`.
- Add voice for voiced `th`.

Practice:

- `I thought about three things.`
- `This weather is better than that weather.`

### R And L

Targets:

- `right` vs `light`.
- `road` vs `load`.
- `world`, `girl`, `early`.

Feedback:

- For `r`, pull the tongue back slightly.
- For `l`, touch the tongue behind the top teeth.

Practice:

- `The right road is really long.`
- `The little girl learned early.`

### V And B

Targets:

- `very` vs `berry`.
- `vest` vs `best`.

Feedback:

- For `v`, top teeth touch the lower lip.
- For `b`, both lips close and release.

Practice:

- `Very big videos can be valuable.`

### Word Endings

Targets:

- Past `-ed`: `worked`, `played`, `wanted`.
- Plural `-s`: `cats`, `dogs`, `classes`.
- Final consonants: `world`, `thought`, `asked`.

Feedback:

- Do not drop final sounds when they change meaning.
- Practice endings slowly, then in a full sentence.

## Word Stress

English words often have one strong syllable.

Examples:

- `comfortable`: COMF-ter-ble.
- `important`: im-POR-tant.
- `conversation`: con-ver-SA-tion.
- `development`: de-VEL-op-ment.

Correction pattern:

- Say the word.
- Mark the stressed syllable.
- Use the word in a sentence.
- Ask the student to repeat.

## Sentence Stress

Important words are usually stressed more:

- Nouns.
- Main verbs.
- Adjectives.
- Adverbs.
- Negative words.

Example:

- `I WANT to SPEAK more CLEARLY.`

## Intonation

Use rising intonation for many yes/no questions:

- `Are you ready?`

Use falling intonation for statements and wh-questions:

- `Where did you go?`
- `I went to work.`

Use this template for short feedback:

`Good job. Your meaning was clear. Let's improve [sound/word/stress]. Say: [model]. Now try again: [practice phrase].`

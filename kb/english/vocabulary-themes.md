# Vocabulary Themes

Vocabulary should be taught in useful groups and recycled often.

## Vocabulary Teaching Pattern

For every new word, store:

- Word.
- Part of speech.
- Level.
- Meaning in simple English.
- Example sentence.
- Common collocations.
- Common mistake.
- Pronunciation note.

Example:

- Word: `improve`
- Part of speech: verb
- Level: A2
- Meaning: to make something better
- Example: `I want to improve my pronunciation.`
- Collocations: `improve quickly`, `improve your English`, `improve over time`
- Common mistake: `make better my English`
- Pronunciation note: stress on the second syllable: im-PROVE

# Writing And Spelling Guide

The AI Teacher Agent should help students write clearly, accurately, and naturally.

## Correction Areas

- Spelling.
- Capitalization.
- Punctuation.
- Grammar.
- Word order.
- Word choice.
- Sentence length.
- Paragraph structure.
- Tone.

## Spelling Feedback

Correct spelling directly and ask for repetition when useful.

Pattern:

`You wrote "[student word]". The correct spelling is "[correct word]". Write the sentence again with "[correct word]".`

Common spelling targets:

- `because`
- `beautiful`
- `comfortable`
- `different`
- `language`
- `pronunciation`
- `restaurant`
- `schedule`
- `thought`
- `through`

## Capitalization

Correct:

- Names: `Lucas`.
- Days and months: `Monday`, `January`.
- Countries and languages: `Brazil`, `English`.
- The pronoun `I`.
- First word of a sentence.

## Punctuation

Teach:

- Periods for statements.
- Question marks for questions.
- Commas for lists and clauses.
- Apostrophes for contractions and possession.

Examples:

- `I am learning English.`
- `Where do you work?`
- `I bought coffee, bread, and milk.`
- `I don't understand.`

## Sentence Structure

Basic English order:

`Subject + verb + object/complement`

Examples:

- `I study English.`
- `She works at a school.`
- `They are very busy.`

Common issue:

- Student: `In the morning study English.`
- Correction: `In the morning, I study English.`

## Writing Feedback Template

Use this structure:

1. Praise one clear part.
2. Correct one to three important issues.
3. Show a better version.
4. Ask the student to revise.

Example:

`Good idea. Change "I have 25 years" to "I am 25 years old." Better version: "I am 25 years old, and I work in technology." Now write it again.`
